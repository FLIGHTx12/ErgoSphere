ErgoSphere ARENA - Battle Manual

Overview

Welcome to the ErgoSphere ARENA, a turn-based tactical battle system where two players (Jaybers8 and FLIGHTx12!) team up to defeat powerful monsters from the ErgoSphere universe.

Battle Mechanics

Basic Controls

  • Enter: Attack with current inputs
  • Space Bar: Switch between players
  • Tab: Cycle between attack inputs
  • + Key: Add an attack input
  • - Key: Remove an attack input
  • Mobile Swipe Left/Right: View battle log or monster info

Damage System

  • Regular Damage: Any number not in monster's "hit numbers" deals that damage
  • Special Moves:
    • Jaybers8: Input "8" for 100 damage
    • FLIGHTx12!: Input "12" for 100 damage
  • Counter-Attacks: Monster attacks when you input their hit numbers

Strategy Tips

  1. Check the monster's hit numbers to avoid triggering counter-attacks
  2. Switch players strategically for different special moves
  3. Sometimes fewer, higher-value attacks are better than many small ones
  4. Remember your character's special number (8 or 12) for big damage
  5. Keep an eye on the timer and plan accordingly
