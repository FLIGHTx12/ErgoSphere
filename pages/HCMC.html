HCMC: The Expanded Guide

Alright, buckle up, because the HCMC is a bit more intricate than it looks. Here's the definitive guide to harnessing its power:

I. Getting Started

God Particles: Your Power Source

  • The HCMC runs on raw creative energy called God Particles. You'll see your current balance displayed at the top.
  • Starting Up: To begin, you'll need to input how many God Particles you want to use. Each spin costs 2 God Particles.
  • Earning Particles: God Particles can be obtained through various activities in ErgoSphere (check the game rules for specifics).

Choosing Your Channel

  • Select your desired reality channel:
  • LOOT: For rewards and modifiers.
  • PVP: To select a player-versus-player game.
  • CO-OP: To select a cooperative game.

II. The Spin Cycle

Initiating the Spin:

  • Click the Start button. This consumes 2 God Particles from your balance.
  • The HCMC will begin cycling through potential outcomes.

The Display:

  • Images and descriptions of various options will flash on the main display.
  • In the side panel, you'll see all available outcomes in the current channel.
  • Each outcome has a number of green dots (ðŸŸ¢) indicating its base probability. More dots = higher chance.

Stopping the Spin:

  • Click the Stop button when you see an outcome you like.
  • The HCMC will begin to slow down.

Spin Again vs. Accept

  • After the spin slows, you'll have a choice:
  • Spin Again: Click the "Spin Again" button to re-spin the wheel. This will cost 3 God Particles.
  • Accept: Click the "Accept" button to claim the displayed outcome. This will remove all of your God Particles.

III. Advanced Techniques

Boosting Probabilities

  • In the side panel, each outcome has a small "+" button.
  • Clicking this button temporarily increases that outcome's probability by 5%.
  • Cost: Each boost costs 0.5 God Particles.
  • Stacking: You can boost an outcome multiple times to further increase its chance.
  • Duration: Boosts last only for the current spin cycle.

Understanding Costs

  • Some LOOT outcomes might have a Ducat cost. This cost is deducted after you accept the outcome.

IV. Important Considerations

Particle Management:

  • Carefully manage your God Particles. Spinning too much can leave you empty-handed.
  • Consider boosting outcomes strategically to increase your odds of getting what you want.

Risk vs. Reward:

  • Spinning again gives you another chance but costs more Particles.
  • Accepting guarantees the current outcome but empties your Particle reserves.

Outcome Details:

  • Always check the outcome details in the side panel before accepting.

With this knowledge, you're now a true HCMC master! Use its power wisely, and may the odds be ever in your favor.
