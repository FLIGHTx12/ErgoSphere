BINGWA Challenge Rules

RULES 

Bingwa - Champion 
Kushindwa- Failure
Contestants will play 1 Wii sports (see below) game each Sunday morning to see who comes out as the winner. If your controller isn't working properly or "The game cheated" tough luck. All scores are final. Both contestants have to shake hands at the end of the game. The week of your Birthday (Sunday-Saturday) you are automatically Bingwa. On the 3rd win in a row, the Bingwa shall receive a 100 Ducat bonus from the Bank.

    🏆Kushindwa must cook breakfast for Bingwa on that Sunday and make Dinner the Following Saturday.
    🏆Bingwa gets to enjoy LUXURY HOUR for the first hour of meal prep as well as when the Kushindwa cooks Saturday dinner that week.
    🏆Bingwa gets final veto on the next week's scheduled events and can rearrange anything (no argument or rebuttal)
    🏆Bingwa gets to choose the week's movie, YouTube theater, and errand. (no argument or rebuttal)
    🏆Bingwa gets to veto any one argument that week. They can postpone the argument for a day or week. (no argument or rebuttal)
    -At the end of a calendar year, the contestant with the most weeks as the Bingwa receives the Honorary Yearly Bingwa Reward:
    • Automatic Bingwa the last 2 weeks of the year (over the holiday travel season)
    • 2 Spins of the Loot Wheel
    • Honorary Eregosphere Trophy

🏀 ErgoHOOPS RULES 🏀

Objective: The offensive player tries to score, and the defensive player tries to prevent the offensive player from scoring. 🛡️ The first player to 10 points wins and is allowed to decide the first chore for each player during Saturday cleaning hour. 🧹

Setup:

  • Offensive Player: Starts outside the 3-point line, off from the free-throw line, on either side of the basket. ⛹️‍♀️⛹️‍♂️
  • Defensive Player: Starts on the elbow on the opposite side of the offensive player. 🧍‍♀️🧍‍♂️
    • Handicap: If there is a height advantage, the defensive player is allowed to start behind the first hash from the free-throw line. 📏

Rules:

  • All three-point shots are worth 2 points. 🏀
  • All other shots are worth 1 point. ⛹️‍♀️⛹️‍♂️
  • No bounce passes are allowed. 🚫
  • Must win by 2 points. ✌️
  • The offensive player must say the score and check the ball before starting. 🗣️
    • The offensive player is allowed to confirm the score before stating it, but if either checking the ball or stating the score is not done or the wrong score is stated, the offensive player forfeits their turn. ❌
  • The offensive player can drive for a layup, take a jump shot, or shoot from the 3-point line where they are starting. ⛹️‍♀️⛹️‍♂️
  • Once the offensive player starts dribbling, the defensive player can leave their position and defend. 🏃‍♀️🏃‍♂️
    • If the defender leaves their starting spot before the offensive player starts dribbling, the offensive player is allowed a free throw and regains possession. 🏀
  • If the offensive player makes their shot, they are given a free opportunity at a three-point shot on the opposite side of where they started. 🔄
    • The defender must wait until the offensive player is set before passing them the ball. ⛹️‍♀️⛹️‍♂️
  • Make It Take It: If the offensive player makes the three-pointer, they get to be on offense again. 🔄
  • If the offensive player misses the three-point shot, then the turn is over and the roles switch. 🔁
  • In the event of a clear/agreed-upon foul, the offensive player is allowed a free turn. ➕

Mods: Players may use mods from the ATLETICO MODS section to enhance or change gameplay. 🚀

Additional Notes:

  • Remember, this game is about having fun and friendly competition with your partner. 😊
  • In the spirit of good sportsmanship, always be respectful of each other and the game's rules. 🤝
  • If any disputes arise, refer back to these rules and discuss them together to find a fair resolution. 💬
⛳ ErgoGOLF RULES ⛳

Objective: Be the first player to reach 10 points and win the game! 🏅 The winner gets to choose the 🏋🏽‍♀️🏋🏽‍♂️ATLETICO CHAMP GROUP WOROUT! on Wednesday morning and decide the first chore for each player during Saturday cleaning hour. 🧹

Setup:

  1. Putting Green: Layout the putting green in the "TV room". 🛋️ Place a mat or something similar to block the golf balls from going under the TV shelf.
  2. Center Line: Find the middle of the putting green and mark it with a weight or similar object along the side. 📏
  3. Chipping Setup: Drape the chipping mat from the laundry table. 🧺 Prop up the chipping grass with the 3lb weight. 🏋️‍♀️
  4. Game Balls: Place the regular golf balls in a chair for participants to choose from during putting. 🏌️‍♀️🏌️‍♂️
  5. Velcro Balls: Each player chooses a velcro ball of their preferred color and places it on the chipping mat. 🔴🔵
  6. Scoreboard: Start with the first 4 boxes checked off on the scoreboard. ☑️

Gameplay:

  1. Starting Player: The youngest player starts the very first game. 👶 For all following games, the loser of the previous ErgoGOLF match starts.
  2. Chipping: Players take turns hitting a velcro ball from the chipping grass. 🏌️‍♀️🏌️‍♂️
  3. Determining Putts: The number the velcro ball lands on corresponds to the number of putts the player gets.
    • If the ball lands on a line between numbers, the player uses the higher number. ⬆️
    • If the ball lands on a negative number, the player's score is reduced, and they don't get any putts for that round. ⬇️
    • If a player moves the chipping grass pad while hitting the velcro ball, their turn is forfeited. 🚫
  4. Putting: The player putts the regular golf ball from behind the middle line on the putting green, aiming for their designated holes. 🕳️ The first player gets to choose which direction they putt first. ➡️⬅️
  5. Scoring: If the player makes a putt, they cover the hole and check off a box on the scoreboard. ☑️ The opponent tracks the number of putts used and is in charge of updating the scoreboard. ✏️
  6. Clarification on Velcro Balls: If all the velcro balls are used, replenish them by taking from the negative zones first, then the 1-point zone, then the 2-point zone, and so on. 🔄

Winning: The first player to reach 10 points wins! 🏆

Mods: Players may use mods from the ATLETICO MODS section to enhance or change gameplay. 🚀

Additional Notes:

  • Remember, this game is about having fun and friendly competition with your partner. 😊
  • In the spirit of good sportsmanship, always be respectful of each other and the game's rules. 🤝
  • If any disputes arise, refer back to these rules and discuss them together to find a fair resolution. 💬
