BINGWA Challenge Rules

RULES 

Bingwa - Champion 
Kushindwa- Failure
Contestants will play 1 Wii sports (see below) game each Sunday morning to see who comes out as the winner. If your controller isn't working properly or "The game cheated" tough luck. All scores are final. Both contestants have to shake hands at the end of the game. The week of your Birthday (Sunday-Saturday) you are automatically Bingwa. On the 3rd win in a row, the Bingwa shall receive a 100 Ducat bonus from the Bank.

REWARDS

    🏆Kushindwa gets to pick next week's game. (cannot be the game they most recently chose)
    🏆Kushindwa must cook breakfast for Bingwa on that Sunday and make Dinner the Following Saturday.
    🏆Bingwa gets to enjoy LUXURY HOUR for the first hour of meal prep as well as when the Kushindwa cooks Saturday dinner that week.
    🏆Bingwa gets final veto on the next week's scheduled events and can rearrange anything (no argument or rebuttal)
    🏆Bingwa gets to choose the week's movie, YouTube theater, and errand. (no argument or rebuttal)
    🏆Bingwa gets to veto any one argument that week. They can postpone the argument for a day or week. (no argument or rebuttal)
    -At the end of a calendar year, the contestant with the most weeks as the Bingwa receives the Honorary Yearly Bingwa Reward:
    • Automatic Bingwa the last 2 weeks of the year (over the holiday travel season)
    • 2 Spins of the Loot Wheel
    • Honorary Eregosphere Trophy

CHALLENGE GAMES

    🏐Volleyball - best of 3
    🏸Badminton - best of 3
    🎾Tennis - best of 3
    ⛳Golf - 3 random holes
    🤺Chambara - best of 3
    ⚽Soccer - 1 game of 4-on-4
    🎳Bowling - 1 standard game
    🎳❓Bowling - 1 special game
-If a Bingwa wins 2 consecutive weeks of the Switch Sports game then the Kushindwa has the right to evoke a "Kushindwa Rights Challenge" unknown and challenge the Bingwa to any 2 player competitive game instead of Switch Sports until the Kushindwa wins. - For Volleyball and Soccer: CPU shall be "strong".
- A player can not choose the game that they most recently picked.
-In the event of a tiebreaker: the game shall be played again.
-In the event of a second tiebreaker: the Kushindwa gets to pick any game from the list of PVP games to determine a winner.
